Top 8 trends of sport shoes are hot now and in the future

Chunky Sneaker trend Chunky sneaker is a design that impresses strongly to sneaker fans in 2018. Chunky sneaker simply means big size shoes, thick soles and strong details ... designed based on Dad Shoes in the 70s. Recently, Adidas and Kanye have also launched their latest Yeezy versions with chunky designs. The product quickly gained significant attention from the Sneakerhead community and global fashion followers, promising to be one of the beautiful sports shoes.

1.Yeezy 700
2.Balenciaga Triple S “Neon Green”
3.M2K Tekno

Diverse materials

For a shoe to look outstanding, the material is an important factor to enhance its stature. The variety of materials is arguably the most noticeable new trend of sneakers. Instead of using the same fabrics and leather as before, tycoons like adidas and Nike tend to use high-tech textile yarns with modern textures to create products that provide high comfort for users.
4.Nike Air Max 1 “Golf Grass”
5.Converse 70s x Tyler The Creator
6.Adidas Deerupt

Trendy white colors

It can be said that the white color or All White is the trend that will last forever. You can coordinate with everything on the person and the white Sneaker will make that outfit more wonderful. "Simple is the best - Every person who has at least one pair of shoes must be white."
7.Air Jordan 4”
8. Futurecraft

Breakthrough in shoe manufacturing technology

The performance of a sports shoe depends largely on the technology that creates it. Along with the development of society, the trend of technology sports shoes is growing strongly. Recently, Nike launched the Adapt BB design - sports shoes with self-lacing technology - with a feature directly linked to the user's smartphone phone.
