Popular sneakers trends in early 2019

Neon colors or Tie-Dye motifs all become the trend on favorite sneakers in 2019.

Chunky sneakers: This is a trend of sneakers starting from 2017 and 2018, with no signs of cooling in 2019 and is expected to be even more explosive. From New York, London, Milan to Paris, everywhere appears classic classic sneakers with striking colors and textures. The shoe, which was a favorite design since the 1970s, is now adapted to the era.

Chunky sneakers are promoted by leading sports brands. Adidas and Kanye West have recently made the fans "excited" when bringing new designs of the mainstream chunky Yeezy.
Diversified materials: In addition to the outstanding designs, the sneakers are also diverse in materials, which experts predict will become the trend in 2019 and 2020. The development of modern production technology has brought New potential for the sports shoe industry with unique materials such as jute, natural grass or transparent plastic.
Fashion house Dior also immersed in the flow of sneakers with unique transparent plastic material, hidden brand logo inside. This is a shoe model promoted by many fashion followers, including stylists and Vietnamese celebrities.
